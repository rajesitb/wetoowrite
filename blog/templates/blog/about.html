About We Too Write

Wading Through Life

Articles on Spreading Information and Wisdom

This Site intends to be a repository of articles, short essays which contain lessons from the journey of life. The readers of these articles may become wiser and approach similar life situations armed with the wisdom and information acquired from these articles.
One article may cover only one important subject and amply draw out lessons
The articles may be published in a series and mentioned accordingly

Encountering Challenges

Articles on Guidance for facing common challenges

The Site intends to be of help to all young men and women who are stepping out of their homes to confront various challenges of life such as choosing a field for further education or choosing a career in a particular field
This dilemma is faced by one and all but there is lack of informed and reliable guidance.
The experience gained by us as we go through our own situations needs to be collated and this Site intends to do the same so that in times to come, reliable and trustworthy guidance and be obtained from this repository

Topical Issues

Articles Covering Current Topics

Current issues generate the most interest. While they are already covered in detail in various publications, the Articles posted here must carry out independent, authentic and in-depth research
It should not just be another publication on the topic of current issues. Your analysis must be unique draw attention to newer aspects on the subject and draw lessons in the spirit of this website.

How to Post

Submitting Articles

Members who have registered with the Website are invited to post their Articles
The members are required to agree to the T&C prior to registration
The Article can be written using the Simple Mark Down Editor available on the Post page. There is help available on the editor for the same. There are handy news and twitter links to delve deep into and research on current topics
The submitted Article will be vetted for abiding by the T&C of the Website. Articles meeting the criteria, will be published within one working day. In case of any aberrations, the same will be informed to the author for suitable corrections and resubmission
The authors can update or delete their articles at any time.
There is a commenting system for each publication. Please login and use the same to give your comments.
